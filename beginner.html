Beginner Program

8 weeks · 3 days per week · Full body focus

Program Overview

The Foundation program is designed for people who are new to structured exercise or returning after a long break. The goal is to build the fundamentals: proper movement patterns, basic strength, and a consistent training habit.

You'll train 3 days per week with full-body workouts, giving your body plenty of time to recover between sessions. By the end of 8 weeks, you'll have a solid base to move into an intermediate program.

Who is this for? Complete beginners, people returning from injury, or anyone who has never followed a structured program before.

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Frequency: 3 days/week
  • Session length: 45-60 minutes
  • Equipment: Dumbbells or gym access
  • Focus: Form, consistency, base strength

Tips for Beginners

  • Focus on form over weight. Use lighter weights until movements feel natural.
  • Rest adequately between sessions. Muscles grow during rest, not during training.
  • Stay hydrated and aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night.
  • Track your workouts so you can see your progress over time.
  • Don't skip warm-ups — 5 minutes of light movement before each session makes a big difference.
