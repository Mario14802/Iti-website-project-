Nutrition

How Much Protein Do You Actually Need?

The fitness industry is obsessed with protein — and for good reason. Protein is the macronutrient most directly linked to muscle growth and retention. But there's a lot of misinformation out there about how much you actually need. Spoiler: it's probably less than the supplement industry wants you to believe.

Current research consistently points to 1.6–2.2 grams per kilogram of bodyweight as the optimal range for muscle building. Going beyond that has diminishing returns for most people.
