Our Story

“Our story began as two engineers with a shared passion for technology and fitness. As part of the ITI Software Testing Intake 46, we created M&Y as our web fundamentals project using HTML and CSS. This platform reflects our vision of combining a healthy lifestyle with simple, user-friendly design. M&Y stands for Youssef & Mario — a journey of growth, discipline, and innovation.”
