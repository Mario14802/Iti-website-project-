Advanced Program

12 weeks · 5 days per week · Push / Pull / Legs Split

Program Overview

The Unleashed program is built for experienced lifters who have a strong training foundation and are ready to push for peak performance. This 12-week program uses a Push/Pull/Legs split run twice per week for high volume and maximum stimulus.

Expect challenging sessions, advanced techniques like drop sets and rest-pause, and a structured deload week every 4 weeks to prevent burnout and maximize long-term gains.

Who is this for? Lifters with 1+ year of consistent training, comfortable with compound movements and who can push near failure safely.

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Frequency: 5 days/week
  • Session length: 75–90 minutes
  • Equipment: Full gym required
  • Focus: Volume, intensity, peak performance

Deload Protocol (Weeks 4, 8, 12)

Every 4th week is a deload week. Reduce all weights by 40–50% and all volume by half. Keep movement quality high. This is not a rest week — it's a recovery week that ensures you can push hard in the following mesocycle.

Important: Never skip deload weeks. They are when your body consolidates strength gains and repairs connective tissue.
