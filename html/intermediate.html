Intermediate Program

10 weeks · 4 days per week · Upper / Lower Split

Program Overview

The Momentum program is built for trainees who have at least 2–3 months of consistent training under their belt. At this stage, you're ready to increase training frequency, add volume, and start lifting heavier with more structured progression.

This 10-week program uses an upper/lower body split, allowing you to target muscle groups twice per week for optimal growth and strength gains.

Who is this for? Those who have completed the Beginner Program or have 2–3 months of consistent gym experience.

  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Frequency: 4 days/week
  • Session length: 60–75 minutes
  • Equipment: Full gym access required
  • Focus: Progressive overload, split training

Progressive Overload Guidelines

At the intermediate level, planned progression is essential. Follow these rules to keep improving:

  • Add 2.5–5 kg to compound lifts (squat, deadlift, bench) every 1–2 weeks when you complete all reps with good form.
  • Add 1–2 reps per set before increasing weight on isolation exercises.
  • Keep a training log every session — track weights, sets, and reps.
  • If you fail to complete the target reps for 2 sessions in a row, reduce weight by 10% and build back up.
